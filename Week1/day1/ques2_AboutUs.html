About Us

Welcome to True Now, your trusted source for clear, factual, and impactful news. We aim to provide information that empowers you to understand the world better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make news simple, accessible, and unbiased. In an age of fast-changing headlines, we strive to bring you thoughtful reporting and meaningful stories.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We verify every story before publishing.
  • Transparency: Open and clear sourcing of information.
  • Clarity: News presented in a way everyone can understand.
  • Innovation: Using technology to deliver news effectively.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, True Now started with a small team of passionate journalists and creators who wanted to change the way news is delivered. Our goal has always been to create a space where readers can find authentic information without noise or distractions.

Today, True Now has grown into a digital-first news platform covering a wide range of topics including world events, technology, sports, culture, and more.

Meet Our Team

We are a diverse group of writers, reporters, editors, photographers, and researchers. Each team member brings unique experience and perspective, helping us deliver balanced and inclusive news coverage.

Why Choose True Now?

With so much misinformation out there, True Now focuses on facts and context. We help you cut through the noise so you can make informed decisions about what matters most.
